A friendly guide for kids and teens to find royalty-free images for school projects and creative work

1 Understand Copyright Rules

Not all images online are free to use. Look for ones labeled:

  • Royalty-free - No payment needed to use them
  • Creative Commons - Usually free with some rules
  • Public Domain - Free for anyone to use
2 Use Safe Search Features

Many search engines and websites have special filters:

  • Turn on "Safe Search" in Google or Bing
  • Look for "Filter" options on image sites
  • Use websites made especially for kids

3 Downloading and Using Images

When you find an image you like:

  1. Click the "Download" button (usually near the image)
  2. Choose a size (medium is good for most projects)
  3. Save to your computer in a folder you'll remember
  4. Check if you need to credit the creator (some sites require this)
